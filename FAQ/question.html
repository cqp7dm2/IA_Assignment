Here are some most frequently asked question...

How long will i wait to receive my photos ?

The sample gallery will send to you within 4 days. The full gallery will sent to you two weeks after photo shooting session.

We always try to do our best to deliver your photos to you as soon as possible.

What right do i have to the soft copy of the photo ?

You have the right to reprint photos whenever you wnat. But, you may not sell your images for profit or publish your images without written consent of APU Photography Club.

Any commercial use of the photos is not allowed if not separately agreed on. The copyright of the images will with the APU Photography Club.

How much is the deposit should i pay ?

For the deposit to confirm your booking is RM300. The remainder is due two weeks before the shooting session.

For easy payment for you, we also offer several online payment option and paypal is available.

Can i get my deposit back if i cancel the booking ?

We are sorry to inform that you will not receive your deposit back.The deposit will covers any loss in the form of lost bookings and eventual cost that arisen before the actual photo session

However, we understand that unpredictable situation will happen, so we will try to work on alternatives if possible.

What should i prepare for the photo shooting session ?

Good mood and a wonderful smile is the most important thing. Makesure you have enough time to prepare for the shooting session so that you will not become nervous when taking photos.

Comfortable cloth fitting with the theme of shooting and some make up are well welcome

How do i reserve you for a photo shooting ?

After we have decided on a date and time for you, you can pay the required deposit and signed contract with us.

Once we have the contract and deposit, your booking are officially reserved .

Do you have liability insurance ?

Yes,we have public liability cover together with professional indemnity and insurance on our equipment.

What is your shooting style ?

We love candid moments and real emotions that present by our customer.

In our shooting session, we focus on the interactions of you and try to give you somee instruction to guide you if necessary.
